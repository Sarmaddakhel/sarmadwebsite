Who is sanji?

Sanji is one of the main characters in the popular manga and anime series "One Piece" created by Eiichiro Oda. He is part of the Straw Hat Pirates, serving as the crew's cook and a skilled martial artist.

Sanji's cooking skills are central to his character. He was trained in the culinary arts by Zeff, a legendary pirate chef who also taught him martial arts. Sanji's cooking ability is not only exceptional but is also a fundamental part of how he contributes to the crew.
