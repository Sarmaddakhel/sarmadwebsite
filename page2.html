The Recipe for Meat on the bone

Ingredients: Beef ribs, salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, barbecue sauce

Recipe:

  1. Season beef ribs with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  2. Grill the ribs on medium-high heat until cooked through
  3. Brush with barbecue sauce and cook for an additional 5-10 minutes.
  4. Serve hot.

Enjoy your meal <3
