The Recipe for All blue seafood pasta

Ingredients: Spaghetti, clams, shrimp, scallops, cherry tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, white wine, parsley.

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a large pan, saut√© garlic in olive oil until golden.
  3. Add cherry tomatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Pour in white wine and add clams, shrimp, and scallops; cook until seafood is done.
  5. Toss in cooked spaghetti and chopped parsley. Serve hot.
