The Recipe for Seafood Risotto

Ingredients: Arborio rice, shrimp, squid, mussels, white wine, fish stock, garlic, onion, olive oil, butter, parsley.

The Recipe:

  1. In a large pan, saut√© garlic and onion in olive oil until translucent
  2. Add Arborio rice and cook until slightly toasted.
  3. Pour in white wine and let it evaporate.
  4. Gradually add fish stock, stirring continuously.
  5. Add shrimp, squid, and mussels; cook until seafood is done.
  6. Stir in butter and chopped parsley before serving.

Enjoy your meal <3
